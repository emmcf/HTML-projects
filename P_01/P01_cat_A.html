Shaker

Essential tool in mixology used to mix and chill the ingredients of a cocktail.

Bar spoon

A long, thin tool used to mix ingredients in a mixing glass. Its twisted handle facilitates the stirring motion and can also be used to measure ingredients and create layers in cocktails.

Jigger

Double-sided measuring cup used to measure precise amounts of liquids and ensure consistency and accuracy when preparing cocktails. It has two cones of different sizes joined at the base, allowing different volumes to be measured.

Bar strainer

It is used to filter solid ingredients and ice from cocktails when pouring them into the serving glass.

Bar masher

A tool used to crush ingredients such as fruits, herbs and spices at the bottom of a glass. This helps release their flavours and aromas, essential for the preparation of cocktails such as the mojito.
